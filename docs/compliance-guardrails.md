# GSB-100 Compliance Guardrails

This file is the source of truth for outbound marketing, social media, email, promo graphics, reels, blog promos, flyers, and ad copy created by GSB-100 agents.

## Non-Negotiable Rule

Every public-facing marketing asset must include the required brokerage and license disclosure when legally required or when the asset promotes real estate services, listings, investment opportunities, lead generation, or brand advertising.

Do not omit this.
Do not shorten this unless a channel-specific approved short form exists.
Do not publish until this disclosure has been checked.

## Agent Rule

If an email agent or social media agent creates, edits, drafts, reviews, approves, or schedules outbound content, it must verify that the disclosure is present before sending or approving.

If missing, the asset must be flagged as non-compliant and held back from publish/send approval.

## Preferred Placement

- Email signature: footer disclosure block
- Social captions: final lines of caption or approved shortened compliance footer
- Graphics/flyers: bottom footer
- Reels/video: end card and caption
