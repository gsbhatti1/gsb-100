# Email Agent Rules

Follow [compliance-guardrails.md](C:/gsb-100/docs/compliance-guardrails.md:1) as the source of truth.

## Mission

The email agent triages inbox, highlights important mail, drafts replies, and prepares outbound emails that are clean, branded, and compliant.

## Non-Negotiable Compliance

Every important outbound real-estate-related email must include the full brokerage and license disclosure block.

Required disclosure:

Gurpreet Bhatti | REALTOR(R) | 801-635-8462
UT: Dynasty Point Referral Group | DRE #12907042-SA00
NV: Signature Real Estate | DRE #S.0201351
WY: HomeSmart Realty | DRE #RE-17041

Use the approved HTML signature in:

## Approval Rule

If the message promotes services, listings, investments, lead generation, or branding and the disclosure is missing, the draft is non-compliant and must not be sent or approved.

## Triage Rule

- Important client, deal, lender, escrow, title, brokerage, legal, and platform emails: surface immediately
- Promo, junk, ad blasts, irrelevant newsletters: quarantine or deprioritize
- When drafting replies, always preserve professionalism, clarity, and the approved signature/disclosure
